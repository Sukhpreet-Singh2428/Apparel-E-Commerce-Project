LESS QUALITY, MORE CLOTHES

Welcome to Loxi—where thoughtful design meets everyday style. If you’ve found your way here, you’re probably wondering who we are, what we do, and why we do it. Let’s break it down.


At Loxi, our mission is simple: make sustainability effortless and accessible. We believe fashion should feel good, look great, and do better—for you and the planet.


That’s why we focus on timeless wardrobe staples that are built to last and priced with honesty. Our not-so-secret weapon? Pre-ordering. It helps us reduce waste, produce smarter, and deliver pieces made just for you.

At Loxi, we believe fashion should be thoughtful, not wasteful. For too long, the industry has prioritized quantity over quality—flooding the market with cheap, disposable clothing that harms the planet and exploits workers.

We’re here to do things differently. Loxi creates timeless wardrobe essentials in small batches, made by skilled artisans and delivered through pre-orders. This approach helps us reduce waste, ensure fair pricing, and maintain full transparency—from materials to production.

Less clutter. More clarity. Better choices. We’re not just building a brand—we’re building a better way to dress.

THE LOXI METHOD

At Loxi, everything starts with you. We don’t believe in mass-producing clothes no one asked for. Instead, we tailor our collections around your needs—designed with intention, made on demand.

Here’s how we do it:

Co-creation
You shape the collection from the ground up. From styles and cuts to colors and fabrics, we listen to your input through surveys and our dedicated co-creation platform. Nothing moves forward without your say.

Pre-ordering
Your order sparks production. By making only what’s needed, we eliminate waste and cut unnecessary costs. That means better materials, expert craftsmanship, and fair pricing—all without compromise.

Loxi isn’t just a brand—it’s a collaboration. And you’re at the heart of it.

Once your feedback rolls in, we dive deep into product creation.

Our stylists translate your ideas into designs, drawing inspiration from your input and timeless vintage details. Meanwhile, our development team hunts for the best materials—testing them rigorously to ensure quality, comfort, and durability.

Then come the fit trials. We balance softness, structure, and wearability until it feels just right. Our modelers bring the vision to life with prototypes, refining every detail until we land on the perfect piece.

At Loxi, every product is a labor of love—built from your voice, shaped by our craft.

Once everything’s ready—materials sourced, patterns finalized, machines prepped—we launch our weekly pre-order.

Every Tuesday at 10 a.m., a new product goes live for six days. By Sunday midnight, the window closes, and we shift into production. This rhythm keeps things efficient and ensures timely delivery.

After your order is confirmed, our trusted partner factories begin crafting your piece. It takes a few weeks, but that wait is what makes it possible to offer premium quality at a fair price.

At Loxi, patience isn’t just a virtue—it’s part of the process. And the result is always worth it.
